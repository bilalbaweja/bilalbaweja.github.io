Pomegranate Citrus Juice


Load up on anti-oxidants with this potent juice mix!

Pomegranates have been used around the world for their medicinal properties. It is indeed a super fruit when it comes to nutritional value. Now you can enjoy pomegranate in the form of juice. The pomegranate citrus juice is the perfect cocktail to ensure that you are getting adequate dose of antioxidants. The variety of fruits uses make this an exciting and colorful juice blend.

Nutrition

150 Calories
3g Fiber
Vitamis A, C, E
Antioxidants galore

Recipe

Use a citrus press or a juicer to juice the grapefruits, oranges, tangerines, and lime. Juice the pomegranates (2/3 cup juice) into a separate bowl. Divide citrus juice among 3 glasses. Top with pomegranate juice, which will settle to the bottom, and serve.

Ingredients required:

  • 2 grapefruits
  • 2 oranges
  • 2 tangerines
  • 1 lime
  • 2 pomegranates
