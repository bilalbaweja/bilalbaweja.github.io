Orange Carrot Juice


Beat the cold with this Vitamin Tonic

The orange carrot juice is perfect for a warm summer day. Its balanced flavors not only rejuvenate your body but also provide the much needed vitamin A and D for your body. It is a must have for folks with weak immune systems. It has been tried and tested by many medical professionals. Regular intake with a zest of ginger keeps flu and cold away for the whole year. Do yourself a favor and save yourself and your family some sick days!!

Nutrition

Recipe

Making orange carrot juice has never been easier. Just follow the recipe below precisely, and you won't go wrong. First take two apples, pit them, and cut them into chunks. Second, take 2 carrots and break them into 2-3 pieces. Next take some ginger, and grate it into tiny pieces. Now take all the ingredients plus some ice and put it all together in a blender. Blend for 2 minutes and enjoy!!

Ingredients required:

  • 2 apples
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • Ice-cubes
