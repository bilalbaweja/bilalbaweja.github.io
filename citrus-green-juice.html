Citrus Green Juice


Give your mornings a refreshing start!!

How many times have you woken up in the morning groggy, taken some caffiene, felt the crash, and repeated the cycle. Thankfully, there is a way out...

A morning juice with fresh ingredients is the perfect way to start the day. Lemon and lime juice go well with flavorful greens and spicy celery. If you prefer your juice a little sweeter, add an extra orange or an apple.

Nutrition

100 Calories
5g Fiber
Vitamis A, C, E

Recipe

Using a centrifugal juice, feed in the spinach, lemons and lime. Add the oranges and celery stalk. Stir. Add ice if desired and serve. I recommend using lemons and limes from Paramount Citrus. They are consistently fresh and juicy and available at most major grocery, mass and club retailers..

Ingredients required:

  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 lemons peeled
  • 1 lime peeled
  • 2 oranges peeled
  • 1 celery stalk
  • Ice-cubes
